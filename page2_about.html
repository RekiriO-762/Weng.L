The future of robotics is not about replacing Humans but augmenting them

With the advancement of knowledge in the technology sector and the rise of AI in the public eye at Weng.I, we believe that through the combination of AI and Robotics we can improve the lives of all on Earth, for example, helping the development of machines that create detailed artificial limbs in minutes or complete complex surgeries that even the best doctors would balk at.

We believe this can be achieved through the use of AI. Why we decided to create a company focused on this goal is because we saw the potential of AI and how it can be used to test and enhance our creation processes and how the robotics industry would greatly benefit from having AI as a helper, as such the time it would take to create such groundbreaking tech would be cut in half furthering our goal to make the lives of all on this world we call home fruitful, so humanity can live without restraint and die knowing they had no regrets caused by cheap and fixable issues.
